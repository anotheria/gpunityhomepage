About us

GPUnity is a Seattle-based company building software solutions for for GPU-based tasks like mining, video-rendering and machine learning

What we do

GPUnity Mining Optimizer was designed to increase mining returns in a 30-50% range by autonomously switching GPU miners to mine the most profitable digital asset based on the current pricing and difficulty data. For AMD we provide a specialized solution, which increases the cards profitability by 10-25 %.
