About us

GPUnity is a Seattle-based company building software solutions for GPU-based tasks like mining, video-rendering and machine learning.

Products

Mining Optimizer

GPUnity MiningOptimizer was designed to increase mining returns in a 30-50% range by autonomously switching GPU miners to mine the most profitable digital asset based on the current pricing and difficulty data.

AIOptimizer

GPUnity AIOptimizer was designed to optimize the deployment of AI/HPC tasks across a decentralized GPU network with a focus on efficiency and accessibility of AI and HPC workflow.

Video Rendering

We are equipped to distribute video rendering tasks to our decentralized GPU network.

Ellen Polsky

Ellen is a woman on a mission and CEO at GPUnity.

Ellen is the CEO at GPUnity and has a 10+ year record of achievement. Her medical background as a pediatric dental surgeon has equipped her with a sharp and analytical mind, which manifests in her track record of building and running highly successful companies. Her experience ranges from founding to mergers and acquisitions across several industries, including blockchain and healthcare.

Being an entrepreneur is second nature to her and she has been perfecting her skills for several years now. Ellen has held various positions such as member and co-founder of Alloy Blockchain Solution, an innovative fintech platform, founder and CEO of VP Surgery Center, a medical surgical facility, as well as co-founder and partner at Visio, a health care service company, which manages multiple pediatric oral surgery and orthodontic facilities in the Greater Seattle Area since 2009.

At VP surgery center, she remains on the board of directors, after the company’s successful exit to a MultiCare health care organization.

When she is not in the midst of building yet another successful company she loves to walk her two dogs, explore Seattle’s culinary scene with her friends and spend time with her husband, a head and neck surgeon turned entrepreneur.
